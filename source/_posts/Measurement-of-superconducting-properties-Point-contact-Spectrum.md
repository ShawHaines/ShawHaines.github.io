# Superconducting Energy Gap

BCS theory predicts

$$
k_BT_C=1.14\hbar\omega_De^{-1/N(0)V}
$$

$$
2\Delta(0)=3.53 k_BT_C
$$

where $T_C$ is the critical temperature of a superconductor, and $\Delta$ is the superconducting energy gap.
